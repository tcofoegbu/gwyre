Gwyre Project

The home of the future resource under development

The GWYRE resource will contain structures of proteins and protein complexes from model organisms, annotated by phenotypic effects of genetic mutations. The resource will allow search by organism, keyword, sequence, and structure of the proteins. Residues in the proteins will be scored by the effect of changing the wild type to other amino acids

The project is a collaboration of Vakser Lab, The University of Kansas and Sternberg Lab, Imperial College London

Dataset Download

 

The project develops advances methodologies and data resources for genome-wide modeling of proteins, protein interactions, and assessment of phenotypic effects of genetic mutations.
